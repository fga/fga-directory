Occupy Atlanta began at Woodruff Park (that we "rechristened" Troy Davis Park) as our public forum. It has been a enduring struggle to preserve that location. We have several occupations throughout the city of Atlanta, but our main headquarters is currently above the Peachtree & Pine Atlanta Task Force for the Homeless on the Fourth Floor.

General Assembly currently takes place on Wednesdays at 7PM and Saturdays at 3PM. There are also sometimes emergency GA's that are held. Refer to the OA Website to find the location of any particular GA.
